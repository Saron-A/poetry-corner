Welcome to my poetry corner!

I hope you enjoy all the stuff I have written so far and will continue to write . Here's a little introduction about myself; My name is Saron Abebe. I am 22 years old and I live in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. I have been writing poems since I was a little kid.However, I believe my writing met its turning point when I turned 16 and started reading different books and poetry. Exploring literatures from various authors opened a new dimension where I got exposed to different topics I can write about and expanded my imagination as well as vocabulary.My collection include poems of various genres such as romance, tragedy, adventure, and more. Feel free to reach out or give feedback on my content.
