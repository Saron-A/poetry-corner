Wait For Me Till I get home

By: Saron A.

To tell you of my tears
The cause being me
To erupt as a volcano
Been dormant for years
Suddenly breaking out with all this malice
To cry before you
And let the tears tell you of my days
my nights
My life so far
Since we last seen
Well I've been on a train going downhill
You'd know since you see everything
When you search my heart what do you see
the bitterness and darkness
Yet what is beneath ?
I feel shallow
I see myself headed towards an inescapable pit
Where vipers glide over one another
Intertwine their bodies
Open their mouth to receive me
And tear me apart
Hopping on the devil's trampoline
Like he tossed me high on purpose
Towards the west in the middle of the day
And when the sun set
I sank in through the dark
Onto my deathbed
My headstone smeared by the sanctity I wasted
Blood resurfacing from the dirt
I realized I should have let up my life
When I had the chance to see your holy gates
To hear the angels' halos
Now all I see is the scarlet of flames
Waiting to blaze my soul
The magots
Waiting to burrow my bones
You
Waiting to cast me out with the demons
The love I once saw in your eyes
Replaced by disappointment and disdain
I deserve it all
Your wrath is my justified fate
Yet I hope you know my regrets
How remorseful those day without you have been.
