Bows and Arrows

By: Saron A.

I wished on fairies
for you.

I wore my heart on my sleeves,
and gave it away to anyone new.

But I was searching for you
in millions of eyes.

I was scrutinizing every iris,
for that peculiar shade of yoursâ€”

for that hue that made me fall.

I tried to kill this love,
so it could reincarnate in a different heart.

But I guess I'm bound to fall for you
in every life.

For a hint of your scent has blended with my soul,
and each rebirth feels like a chain of thorns,

stinging this sore heart,
deprived of you.
